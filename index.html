Why ACO Walls

Every wall
is an
upgrade.

ACO Walls are hollow-core precast non-loadbearing walls manufactured with M25 grade concrete under strict quality control — every panel leaves our Maheshwaram factory dimensionally precise, with a consistent flat finish and no site variability.

01

Zero Plastering

Factory-precise flat surfaces mean walls are ready for putty and paint directly — no thick plastering, no curing, no waiting.

02

Built-in MEP Conduits

Hollow cores running the full panel length naturally route electrical wiring, water pipes, and HVAC — no chasing or drilling needed.

03

More Carpet Area

At 92–120mm, ACO panels are substantially thinner than equivalent brick walls — giving builders measurably more usable floor area per floor plate.

04

2 Hr Fire Rated

ACO Wall panels are 2 hours fire rated as per IS Code — independently tested and confirmed. Essential for hospitals, commercial towers, schools, and high-rise residential buildings.

05

Superior Sound Insulation

Hollow-core concrete achieves 41 dB (92mm) and 43 dB (120mm) sound reduction — quieter homes, more private offices, and compliant hotels and healthcare buildings.

06

Moisture Resistant

Concrete doesn't rot, mould, or degrade under humidity — ideal for wet rooms, high-rainfall environments, and coastal construction sites.

From Factory to Floor

From raw material
to ready wall.

01

Factory Production

Concrete is batched, mixed, and extruded on our Acotec line at Maheshwaram — producing panels with consistent dimensions and smooth surfaces every cycle.

02

Quality Control

Every panel is checked for dimensional accuracy, surface quality, and core integrity before being stacked and wrapped for dispatch from our stockyard.

03

Site Delivery

Panels are dispatched directly to your Hyderabad project site with delivery challans. Our stockyard dispatch is coordinated with your floor-by-floor installation schedule.

04

Installation

Panels are lifted upright, cemented to floor and ceiling, joints sealed with PU foam, mortar packed at base — then ground and flushed. Ready to finish.
